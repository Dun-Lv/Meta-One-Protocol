# Meta One Protocol · Allocation Protocol

## 元一系统 · 资金制度

---

### 0. Funding Source
### 0. 资金来源

The system does not presuppose a single source of funding. All funds flowing into the system — whether from member contributions, external donations, returns on collective projects, or other forms of value creation — are pooled into a unified treasury before being allocated according to the rules below. The source of each inflow is recorded and publicly auditable.

系统不预设单一的资金来源。所有流入系统的资金——无论是来自成员贡献、外部捐赠、集体项目收益，还是其他形式的价值创造——统一汇入公共资金池，再按以下规则进行分配。每一笔流入的来源均被记录并公开可审计。

---

### I. Three Types of Funds
### 一、三类资金

#### 1. Equal Distribution Fund (Subsistence Guarantee)
#### 1. 平均分配资金（生存保障）

- **Nature**: Unconditional, equal for all members.
- **性质**：无条件、人人等额。
- **Source**: A proportion of the total system output, deducted as first priority.
- **来源**：系统总产出中按一定比例计提。
- **Proportion**: Adjustable. Each community determines and periodically reviews the proportion through the Dynamic Consensus Engine based on local conditions.
- **计提比例**：可调。由各社区根据当地实际情况，通过动态共识引擎议定比例，并定期复审。
- **Floor Protection**: Regardless of proportion adjustments, the absolute amount must never fall below the **Dignity Baseline**. This baseline is locked by universal consensus and can only be raised, never lowered.
- **下限保护**：无论比例如何调整，调整后的绝对金额不得低于**生存尊严基线**。该基线由全体共识锁定，只升不降。
- **Distribution**: Monthly, automatic, no application required.
- **分配方式**：按月发放，自动到账，无需申请。
- **Firewall**:
  - Cannot be reduced or frozen by any vote.
  - The reserve may only be touched with 100% unanimous consent — this clause stands as a theoretical monument, designed to drive system optimization rather than actual execution.
- **防火墙**：
  - 不可被任何投票削减或冻结。
  - 仅当全体成员 100% 同意时可动用存量——此条款为理论丰碑，系统应永不触发。

---

#### 2. On-Demand Allocation Fund (Development Fuel · Democratic Consensus Track)
#### 2. 按需分配资金（发展燃料 · 民主共识轨道）

- **Share**: Each member holds `1/N` (N = current total membership).
- **份额**：每人拥有 `1/N`（N = 当前总人数）。
- **Individual Discretionary Cap per Proposal**: `1/N`. Exceeding this requires collective approval.
- **单人单次自由裁量上限**：`1/N`，超出须走集体同意程序。
- **Purpose**: Supporting proposals and fulfilling development needs.
- **用途**：支持提案、满足发展需求。
- **Allocation Method**:
  - Upon proposal approval, all members who voted in favor **equally split** the agreed amount from their respective On-Demand shares to fund the proposer.
- **分配方式**：
  - 提案通过后，所有投同意票的成员，从各自的按需份额中**平均分摊**约定金额给提案人。
- **Records**: Monthly ledger for each member detailing amounts used and remaining balance. Fully transparent and auditable.
- **记录**：每人每月动用的金额、剩余额度，逐笔记录，按月公开。

---

#### 3. Chaos Fund (Development Fuel · Minority Innovation Track)
#### 3. 混沌资金（发展燃料 · 少数创新轨道）

- **Share**: Each member holds `1/N` (N = current total membership).
- **份额**：每人拥有 `1/N`（N = 当前总人数）。
- **Individual Discretionary Cap per Proposal**: `1/N`. Exceeding this requires collective approval.
- **单人单次自由裁量上限**：`1/N`，超出须走集体同意程序。
- **Purpose**: Supporting non-consensus, high-risk, minority-driven initiatives that are not widely understood.
- **用途**：支持非共识、高风险、不被多数人理解的个人行动。
- **Allocation Method**:
  - Proposers may directly draw from their own Chaos share to launch projects without prior approval from others.
- **分配方式**：
  - 提案人可直接动用自己名下的混沌份额启动项目，无需事先获得他人同意。
- **Records**: Monthly ledger for each member detailing amounts used and remaining balance. Fully transparent and auditable.
- **记录**：每人每月动用的金额、剩余额度，逐笔记录，按月公开。

### II. Determining Fund Flow: Voting Outcome
### 二、资金流动的决定因素：投票结果

| Voting Outcome    | Available Funds                                           | Note                                                          |
|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| 投票结果              | 可动用的资金                                                    | 备注                                                            |
| Proposal Approved | All Approvers' On-Demand shares (equal split)             | Enters On-Demand track; backed by collective consensus        |
| 提案通过              | 所有同意者的按需份额（平均分摊）                                          | 进入按需分配通道，集体共识支持                                               |
| Proposal Rejected | Only the Proposer's own On-Demand share + own Chaos share | Personal persistence track; does not consume public consensus |
| 提案被否决             | 仅提案人自己的按需份额 + 自己的混沌份额                                     | 个人坚持通道，不消耗公共共识                                                |

**Key Rules**:
- An approval vote means real contribution from one's On-Demand share.
- A rejection vote means no financial contribution, but does not prevent the proposer from proceeding with personal funds.
**关键规则**：
- 同意票 = 真金白银从自己按需份额里掏钱。
- 反对票 = 不出钱，但不阻止提案人用自己的钱继续干。

### III. Process Requirement
### 三、是否需要走流程

| Scenario                                             | Process Required?                                 |
|------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| 情况                                                   | 是否走流程                                             |
| Using only personal funds (On-Demand + Chaos shares) | **No**, proceed directly                          |
| 只动用自己的钱（按需份额 + 混沌份额）                                 | **不需要**，直接干                                       |
| Using others' funds (Collective On-Demand pool)      | **Yes**, must go through Dynamic Consensus Engine |
| 动用别人的钱（集体按需池）                                        | **必须走**动态共识引擎                                     |

### IV. Dynamic Consensus Engine (Summary)
### 四、动态共识引擎（简要）

1. **Practice First**: Any proposal may immediately conduct small-scale practice that does not harm the interests of opponents.
   **先实践**：提案可立即进行不损害反对者利益的小规模实践。

2. **Dynamic Voting**: One person, one vote. Opposing votes may convert to approval votes, and vice versa.
   **动态投票**：一人一票，反对票可转同意票，反之亦然。

3. **Guarantee Loop**: If 100% consensus is not reached, approvers must modify the proposal or provide interest guarantees; opponents provide constructive feedback. Each member re-evaluates their position.
   **保障循环**：未达 100% 同意时，同意方修改提案或提供利益保障，反对方提供建设性意见，每人重新判断。

4. **Termination Right**: If the proposer casts an opposing vote, the proposal is immediately voided.
   **终止权限**：提案提出者若投反对票，提案当即作废。

5. **Conversion Means Contribution**: Any member switching from oppose to approve must bear the same contribution obligation as initial approvers. The contribution amount is set by the **initial approvers' equal split** and is not recalculated due to later conversions.
   **转票即出资**：任何从反对转为同意的成员，须承担与初始同意者相同的出资义务。出资额以**初始同意者平均分摊的结果**为准，不再因后续转票者加入而重新拉平。

6. **Benefit Equals Consent**: Any opponent who actually benefits from a passed proposal is automatically converted to approval status and bears the corresponding contribution obligation under the Conversion Means Contribution rule.
   **利益即同意**：任何反对者，若实际享受到提案带来的利益，则自动转为同意，并按转票即出资规则承担相应出资义务。

7. **Goal**: Iterate toward 100% consensus.
   **目标**：通过迭代追求 100% 共识。

### V. Emergency Protocol
### 五、紧急协议

- **Trigger**: Life-threatening crisis, automatically activated upon hard evidence. No voting required.
- **触发条件**：生命危机，凭硬证据自动触发，无需投票。
- **Funding Source**: Equal split drawn from all members' On-Demand shares.
- **资金来源**：全体成员的按需份额，按人头平均分摊。
- **Amount**: Sufficient to cover the emergency need, determined by the system based on actual circumstances. Detailed records published post-event.
- **金额**：以覆盖紧急需求为限，由系统根据实际情况核定，事后公开明细。
- **Records**: Each emergency deduction is recorded in every member's monthly On-Demand ledger, alongside regular proposal expenditures.
- **记录**：每一笔紧急救助的扣款，计入每个人的按需份额月度台账，与普通提案的支出并列。

### VI. System Flywheel
### 六、系统飞轮

> Individuals enter with personal goals → Access resources through the system to support practice → Success or failure, process knowledge is open-sourced and becomes collective intelligence → Collective intelligence optimizes next-round decisions and rules.

> 个体以自我目标进入 → 通过系统获取资源支持实践 → 无论成败，过程知识开源，成为集体智慧 → 集体智慧优化下一轮决策与规则。

---

### VII. Summary in One Sentence
### 七、一句话总结

> **Subsistence funds are proportionally deducted, adjustable in rate, with a non-negotiable floor; development funds are individually held — use your own freely, seek consensus for others'; approvers split equally, converters pay the same; beneficiaries auto-convert and contribute; opponents pay nothing but don't block you; emergencies draw equally from all On-Demand shares. Everything recorded monthly, fully transparent and auditable.**

> **生存的钱按比例计提、比例可调、底线不降；发展的钱每人一份、动自己的直接干、动别人的走共识；同意者平均分摊，转同意者照此出资；享受利益者自动转为同意并出资；反对者不出钱但不拦你；紧急救助从全体按需份额平均扣。一切按月记录，全程公开可审计。**
