# 元一协议 | Meta One Protocol
## 📜 序：为什么是“元一”？ | 📜 Preamble: Why "Meta One"?
世界正面临协作效率低下与意义缺失的危机。旧有的权力结构与分配模式已显现疲态。
“元一协议”试图探索一种新的可能性：一个以**绝对平等**为基础，以**过程积累**为核心资产的社会技术实验。
我们试图回答一个问题：如果一切从头再来，人类应该如何协作？

The world is facing a crisis of inefficient collaboration and a lack of meaning. The old power structures and distribution models are showing fatigue.
The "Meta One Protocol" attempts to explore a new possibility: a socio-technological experiment based on **absolute equality**, with **process accumulation** as its core asset.
We attempt to answer one question: If we started from scratch, how should humanity collaborate?

## ⚖️ 元规则 (The Meta Rules) | ⚖️ The Meta Rules
任何系统的运行，都必须建立在一套无需证明的公理之上。

**1. 存在第一 (Existence First)**
*   肉体生存权与心理健康权，是系统提供的绝对底线保障。
*   没有个体的存活与健全，任何宏大的协作都毫无意义。

**2. 价值自赋 (Self-valuation)**
*   个体的价值不由他人的评分或系统的指标决定。
*   系统只负责记录与守护这种自我定义的权利，绝不进行任何形式的“价值审判”。

**3. 过程即价值 (Process as Value)**
*   结果固然重要，但**可被自己审计、可被学习的实践过程**，才是系统最核心的资产。
*   失败的尝试，只要过程真实，其价值远高于平庸的成功。

The operation of any system must be based on a set of axioms that require no proof.

**1. Existence First**
*   The right to physical survival and mental health is the absolute bottom-line guarantee provided by the system.
*   Without the survival and well-being of the individual, any grand collaboration is meaningless.

**2. Self-valuation**
*   The value of an individual is not determined by others' ratings or system metrics.
*   The system is only responsible for recording and protecting this right of self-definition, and absolutely refrains from any form of "value judgment".

**3. Process as Value**
*   Results are important, but the **practice process that can be self-audited and learned from** is the most core asset of the system.
*   Failed attempts, as long as the process is genuine, are far more valuable than mediocre successes.

## ⚙️ 核心机制 (Core Mechanisms) | ⚙️ Core Mechanisms
为了实现上述规则，系统设计了以下三大支柱：

*   **生存保障层 | Survival Guarantee Layer**
    *   **机制 | Mechanism**: 无条件基本收入（UBI）或资源平均分配。Unconditional Basic Income (UBI) or equal distribution of resources.
    *   **目的 | Purpose**: 剥离生存焦虑，让个体回归创造与思考。Strip away survival anxiety, allowing individuals to return to creation and thought.

*   **发展燃料层 | Development Fuel Layer**
    *   **机制 | Mechanism**: 双轨制资金池——“按需分配”（民主共识） + “混沌资金”（保护少数创新）。Dual-track fund pool – "Distribution according to need" (democratic consensus) + "Chaos Fund" (protecting minority innovation).
    *   **目的 | Purpose**: 既保证集体方向的稳健，又保留突破常规的火种。Ensuring the robustness of the collective direction while preserving the spark for breaking conventions.

*   **权力制衡层 | Power Balance Layer**
    *   **机制 | Mechanism**: 一人一票（1人 = 1权）。One person, one vote (1 person = 1 right).
    *   **引擎 | Engine**: 动态共识引擎（见下文）。Dynamic Consensus Engine (see below).

# ⚖️ 权力制衡 | ⚖️ Power Balance
## 根本原则 | Fundamental Principles
- **一人一票**：政治权力绝对平等。**One person, one vote**: Absolute equality of political power.

## 决策核心：动态共识引擎 | Decision Core: Dynamic Consensus Engine
**目标 | Goal**: 通过迭代与动态实践，趋近并达成共识。To approach and reach consensus through iteration and dynamic practice.

### 流程 | Process
1.  **先实践 | Practice First**
    - 提案可立即由**同意方**在**不损害反对者利益**的范围内执行，并产出实践数据。A proposal can be immediately implemented by the **consenting party** within the scope of **not harming the interests of the opponents**, producing practical data.

2.  **动态投票 | Dynamic Voting**
    - 投票状态可自由转换（反对 ⇄ 同意）。Voting status can be freely changed (Oppose ⇄ Agree).
    - 共识状态实时更新与显示。Consensus status is updated and displayed in real-time.

3.  **保障与优化循环 | Guarantee and Optimization Loop**
    - 若未达100%同意，则进入循环：If 100% agreement is not reached, enter the loop:
        - **同意方**须修改提案或提供利益保障方案。**Consenting parties** must modify the proposal or provide an interest guarantee plan.
        - **反对方**可提供建设性意见。**Opposing parties** can provide constructive suggestions.
    - 每轮优化后，**全体成员重新判断**，并更新投票状态。After each round of optimization, **all members re-evaluate** and update their voting status.

4.  **终止权限 | Termination Authority**
    - 提案提出者若投反对票，提案**当即永久作废**。If the proposal proponent votes against it, the proposal is **immediately and permanently voided**.

---
*系统在安全实践中迭代，在动态投票中趋近共识。同意方可行动，反对方受保护，提出者握有最终安全阀。*
*The system iterates in safe practice, approaching consensus through dynamic voting. Consenting parties can act, opposing parties are protected, and the proponent holds the final safety valve.*
