# 五行元理学：以木火土金水为五个元维度（生长性、虚拟性、同构性、边界性、流动性），以生克为结构，以平均为运算，以扰动为输入，构成一个可验证的现实系统框架。

---

## 土（同构性）验证

**实验方法：**

赋予五行五个随机初始数值，按固定顺序执行十步生克操作——木生火、金克木、火生土、水克火、土生金、木克土、金生水、火克金、水生木、土克水。每步操作将参与生或克的两个数值进行平均。重复循环。

**实验结果：**

无论初始数值如何分布，经过有限轮循环后，五个数值必然完全相等。

**结论：**

同构性是五行生克系统的必然归宿。系统自发趋向万物归一的状态。

---

## 金（边界性）验证

**实验方法：**

对照组采用标准一比一权重平均。实验组修改平均权重，例如改为三七开。比较两组收敛结果。

**实验结果：**

权重改变后，收敛值发生偏移，偏移方向与权重偏向一致。权重偏向哪一方，收敛值就朝哪一方移动。

**结论：**

边界条件改变，系统行为可预测地响应。边界性是可调的、可验证的。

---

## 水（流动性）验证

**实验方法：**

运行标准算法，记录每轮每个数值相对于前一轮的变化量。追踪变化量随轮次的演化趋势。

**实验结果：**

变化量每轮按固定比例衰减，呈严格的指数衰减趋势。衰减率稳定，可计算。

**结论：**

信息差可测量、可计算、衰减规律稳定。流动性是系统内在的可量化属性。

---

## 木（生长性）验证

**实验方法：**

运行标准算法，但每轮循环结束后给木固定增加一个微小增量。记录所有维度的演化轨迹，观察哪个维度先响应、哪个维度后被带动。

**实验结果：**

木先上涨，火跟随上涨，土随后上涨，金和水依次响应。偏移路径严格遵循相生顺序传递。

**结论：**

定向扰动沿生克链传递，路径可预测。生长性具有明确的方向性。

---

## 火（虚拟性）验证

**实验方法：**

设定一个阈值。定义规则：每轮循环结束后，若火高于阈值，则可设定触发正向叙事，例如全体数值加一；若火低于阈值，则可设定触发负面叙事，例如全体数值减一。运行两组实验：一组初始火高于阈值，一组初始火低于阈值。比较两组演化轨迹。

**实验结果：**

仅改变初始火这一个条件，两组系统的演化轨迹完全不同。一组从正向循环开始，一组从负向循环开始，系统走向发生根本性分化。

**结论：**

概念和叙事可以作为独立的因果变量，改变系统的走向。虚拟性具有真实的因果效力。

---

## 总体结论

五个维度全部验证通过。任何具备基础编程能力的研究者，均可复现上述实验。

# Five Elements Meta-Theory: Taking Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water as five meta-dimensions (Growth, Virtuality, Homomorphism, Boundary, Fluidity), with generation and overcoming as structure, averaging as operation, and perturbation as input, forming a verifiable framework for modeling reality.

---

## Earth (Homomorphism) Verification

**Experimental Method:**

Assign five random initial values to the five elements. Execute ten sequential steps of generation and overcoming operations in fixed order — Wood generates Fire, Metal overcomes Wood, Fire generates Earth, Water overcomes Fire, Earth generates Metal, Wood overcomes Earth, Metal generates Water, Fire overcomes Metal, Water generates Wood, Earth overcomes Water. Each step averages the two values involved in the generation or overcoming operation. Repeat the cycle.

**Experimental Result:**

Regardless of how the initial values are distributed, after a finite number of cycles, all five values necessarily become exactly equal.

**Conclusion:**

Homomorphism is the inevitable destination of the Five Elements generation-overcoming system. The system spontaneously tends toward a state where all things converge into one.

---

## Metal (Boundary) Verification

**Experimental Method:**

The control group uses standard one-to-one weighted averaging. The experimental group modifies the averaging weight, for example to a three-to-seven split. Compare the convergence results of both groups.

**Experimental Result:**

After the weight is changed, the convergence value shifts, and the direction of the shift aligns with the weight bias. Whichever side the weight favors, the convergence value moves toward that side.

**Conclusion:**

When boundary conditions change, system behavior responds predictably. Boundary is adjustable and verifiable.

---

## Water (Fluidity) Verification

**Experimental Method:**

Run the standard algorithm. Record the change in each value relative to its previous round. Track the evolution trend of these changes across rounds.

**Experimental Result:**

The change amount decays at a fixed ratio each round, exhibiting a strict exponential decay trend. The decay rate is stable and calculable.

**Conclusion:**

Information differential is measurable, calculable, and follows a stable decay pattern. Fluidity is an inherently quantifiable property of the system.

---

## Wood (Growth) Verification

**Experimental Method:**

Run the standard algorithm, but add a fixed small increment to Wood at the end of each cycle. Record the evolution trajectory of all dimensions. Observe which dimension responds first and which follows.

**Experimental Result:**

Wood rises first, followed by Fire, then Earth, with Metal and Water responding in sequence. The deviation path strictly follows the generation order.

**Conclusion:**

Directed perturbation propagates along the generation-overcoming chain. The propagation path is predictable. Growth has clear directionality.

---

## Fire (Virtuality) Verification

**Experimental Method:**

Set a threshold. Define a rule: at the end of each cycle, if Fire is above the threshold, a positive narrative may be triggered, for example adding one to all values; if Fire is below the threshold, a negative narrative may be triggered, for example subtracting one from all values. Run two experiments: one with initial Fire above the threshold, one with initial Fire below the threshold. Compare the evolution trajectories of both groups.

**Experimental Result:**

Changing only the initial condition of Fire produces completely different evolution trajectories between the two systems. One begins a positive cycle, the other begins a negative cycle. The system's direction diverges fundamentally.

**Conclusion:**

Concepts and narratives can function as independent causal variables that alter a system's trajectory. Virtuality possesses genuine causal efficacy.

All five dimensions have passed verification. Any researcher with basic programming skills can reproduce the above experiments.
